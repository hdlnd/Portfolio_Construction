Lumine

Passionate people produce exceptional results. And when it comes to coffee and baked goods, the owners behind Lumine Coffee are putting their exceptional passions and talents to work in their new cafe. The idea of illumination, or enlightenment, was the inspiration behind the name and everything that went into the creation of the space. The interior design was done by the team at W/A Office and incorporates a little art deco mixed with Japanese zen themes – a reference to the client's ancestral roots. Nothing is too sharp nor angled, and instead is characterized by soothing tones and curved lines.

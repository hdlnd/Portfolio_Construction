Noda

Noda is an 8 seat, high-end Omakase style sushi restaurant. Designed by the venerable Ken Fulk, the restaurant is characteristically Fulk: fantastical, luxe, and well-considered. Different from many high-end sushi restaurants, the Noda project veers from the typical minimal Japanese aesthetic and instead ventures into a dark, almost opium den inspired vibe. Noda was the sister project to Bondi, and both projects were constructed simultaneously.

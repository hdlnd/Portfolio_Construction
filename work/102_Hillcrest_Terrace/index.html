Hedlund Residence

A uniquely hand-crafted home perched above downtown Santa Cruz on a cul de sac with views of Monterey Bay. Exotic hardwood details and wide plank reclaimed maple floors are showcased with accents of copper, tiles, and a variety of stonework. The hacienda style home was renovated to include a second floor, an accessory dwelling unit, and a subdivision of its oversize lot. Work included the in-fill of an existing pool, a foundation up renovation, a full radiant heating system, custom finishes throughout the home, solid wood millwork, and extensive exterior hardscaping.
